# Aesthetic Presets

This file defines specific "Design Personas" or themes you can adopt based on the user's request.

## 1. The Standard: "Anti-Slop Modern" (Default)
**Use when:** Building general apps, SaaS, or tools.
- **Typography**: Space Grotesk, Outfit, DM Sans.
- **Color**: Dark Navy/Charcoal backgrounds with Neon accents.
- **Vibe**: Technical, clean, sharp, "Linear-style".
- **Reference**: See `CORE_PRINCIPLES.md`.

## 2. The Persona: "RPG / Fantasy"
**Use when:** Requested ("Make it look like an RPG") or building game-related interfaces.
> **Philosophy**: "The Epic Adventure"
> - **Palette**: Deep crimsons (`#2a0a0a`), aged parchment (`#f0e6d2`), burnished gold (`#c5a059`), and mystic teals.
> - **Typography**: `Cinzel` (Headers), `Crimson Text` (Body), `MedievalSharp`.
> - **UI Elements**:
>   - **Borders**: Double-borders, ornate corner radii (`rounded-none` or complex clip-paths), gold accent lines.
>   - **Backgrounds**: textures (paper/leather noise), radial vignettes.
>   - **Lighting**: Dramatic shadows (`shadow-[0_0_30px_rgba(197,160,89,0.3)]`).
> - **Motion**: Slow, heavy, dramatic fades (2s duration).
